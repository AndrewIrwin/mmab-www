Zoe V. Finkel

Oceanography, Canada Research Chair in Marine Microbial Macroecology

My research interests focus on the eco-physiological controls of phytoplankton community structure, feedbacks between phytoplankton community structure and climate change, and the macroevolutionary history of phytoplankton communities. (Google scholar, Researcher ID B-9626-2008, ORCID 0000-0003-4212-3917, email: zfinkel at dal.ca.)

Andrew J. Irwin

Mathematics & Statistics

I use mathematical and statistical models to study the marine biosphere, phytoplankton growth, and environmental influences on the distribution of phytoplankton in the ocean. (Google scholar, Researcher ID B-2245-2008, ORCID 0000-0001-7784-2319, email: a.irwin at dal.ca.)
